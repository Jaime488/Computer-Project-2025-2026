Type 99

The Type 99 (ZTZ-99) is China’s most advanced third-generation main battle tank, combining heavy firepower, advanced armor, and high mobility. Its 125mm smoothbore gun fires APFSDS, HEAT, and laser-guided missiles, supported by a cutting-edge fire-control system with thermal imaging and hunter-killer capabilities. The tank’s protection includes composite armor, explosive reactive armor (ERA), and an optional laser-based active defense system to disrupt incoming projectiles. Powered by a 1,500-horsepower diesel engine, it reaches speeds of 80 km/h (50 mph), rivaling Western MBTs like the M1 Abrams. The upgraded Type 99A variant features digital networking, improved armor, and enhanced automation, making it the PLA’s frontline heavy tank despite limited exports.

ZTQ-15

The ZTQ-15 (Type 15) is a modern Chinese light tank designed for mobility in rough terrain like mountains and wetlands, weighing around 35 tons for easy transport. It packs a 105mm rifled gun capable of firing APFSDS, HEAT, and guided missiles, balancing firepower with agility. Protected by modular composite armor and explosive reactive armor (ERA), it resists infantry weapons and light anti-tank threats while relying on speed and stealth for survival. Powered by a 1,000-horsepower engine, it hits 70 km/h (43 mph) and excels in high-altitude regions like Tibet. Introduced in 2018, it supports PLA rapid-response units, filling a niche between heavy tanks and infantry fighting vehicles.
